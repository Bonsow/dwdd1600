Who are we?

We are people who want you to touch grass. It can be hard to find places to visit and even harder to find the motivation to go to those places. We hope that people of all ages will find out information and services useful. With any luck we hope that you end up touching some grass.

Here are some of our favorite places with grass to touch

  • Jasper, Alberta Canada
  • Coeur d'Alene, Idaho USA
  • Glacier, Montana USA
  • Bryce Canyon, Utah USA
  • Zion, Utah USA
  • The Grand Canyon, Arizona USA
